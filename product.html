Description

Your flying camera

Introducing Breeze, the flying camera designed with you in mind. Both convenient and compact, Breeze is controlled by your iOS or Android device with the Breeze Cam app and weighs just under 1 pound. Fly indoors or out to capture 4K Ultra High Definition Video & 13MP stills of your next great adventure.

Simply take Breeze out of the box and let it fly

Complex shots made easy

Use one of the flying camera's five automated flight modes to capture unique shots. Use Selfie and Orbit modes to take dramatic aerial group photos and video or use Follow Me mode to grab compelling aerial footage of your action sports adventures. The shots that previously would have required a skilled pilot are now accessible with the click of a button. Soar to the skies and let Breeze do the work.

Safety first

Breeze has optical flow and infrared positioning sensors that let it hold its position indoors and outdoors, making it safe to fly in any situation. And for any reason, you can fly with confidence knowing that Breeze won't drift – no matter your experience or flight expertise. Additionally, there is an auto landing and auto return to home feature – no flight experience is necessary.

Superior image quality

Featuring breathtaking 4K Ultra High Definition and ultra-clear 13 megapixel stills, Breeze is just as capable as drones twice its size. Enjoy a live 720P HD live stream of your flight on your smart device.
